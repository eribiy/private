Prices

No monthly costs

Stylevisit is free to use - you don’t pay any setup or maintenance fee. You only pay for bookings with your salon made on Stylevisit.

Commissions

We collect information about all of your Stylevisit bookings and charge 15% from the total income. No commission is collected if a booking is made through a SV widget placed on your personal website. You only pay commission-fees for bookings made via Stylevisit.
